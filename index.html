About Us

Founded in 2005 by the Ahluwalia Family, Clay Oven offers a vast menu of chicken, lamb, goat, seafood, vegetarian and vegan dishes, as well as a selection of sweets and desserts all prepared from authentic Indian recipes. So whether you have a taste for a flavorful curry or something more exotic, you’ll see why our customers drive far beyond their own neighborhood to taste food the way it’s served in India. Come to Clay Oven today and experience the real thing: memorable food and exceptional value. We also offer delivery service with Mobile Maitre’d, SkipTheDishes.ca and Just-Eat and our online menu is designed to place your order for pickup at any of our locations.
